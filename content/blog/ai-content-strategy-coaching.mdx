Coaching is one of the **hardest creator niches to scale** in our data — and most coaches are making the exact same mistake.

The 188 coaching videos we analyzed averaged **74,447 views**, with a 10.1% stuck rate. That's not bad, but it's nowhere near what finance (1.2% stuck) or cooking (2.8% stuck) is pulling. The problem isn't the niche. It's the default content pattern coaches fall into.

Here's what's actually working.

The top-performing hook in coaching is **Hot Take** — strong opinion, stated directly, upfront. It averaged 198,615 views across 17 videos. That's **4× the niche average**.

Teacher hooks — the default "let me coach you through this" pattern — averaged 46,042. Less than a quarter of what Hot Takes pull.

Example Hot Take hooks that win in coaching:
- *"Stop giving your clients homework. It doesn't work."*
- *"The 'riches are in the niches' advice killed my business for 2 years."*
- *"Coaching certifications are mostly a scam and here's why."*

Each one takes a strong position on something coaches typically believe. Friction creates reach.

The disaster in coaching content: Trust-based videos averaged **2,457 views**. That's 0.03× the niche median.

Curiosity averaged 94K. That's a **38× gap** between the two emotions coaches default to.

Why does Trust underperform so badly? Because coaching creators use it as a proxy for "establishing credibility before getting to the goods." The problem: the algorithm doesn't read credentials. It reads retention, engagement velocity, and categorization speed. Trust-building content gets buried because it doesn't provoke fast enough.

Lead with the provocative idea. Let Trust accumulate across many videos, not within a single one.

## Why coaching gets stuck more than finance

Finance creators default to Investigator + Curiosity (the winning combo in their niche). Coaching creators default to Teacher + Trust (the losing combo in theirs). That one pattern difference explains most of the gap between a 1.2% stuck rate and a 10.1% stuck rate.

## Length: go 60-90 seconds

Coaching median was 63 seconds in our data. Stretch toward the longer end. Coaching content needs:

- The setup (3-5s)
- The reframe / claim (10-15s)
- The reasoning / evidence (20-40s)
- The takeaway (10-15s)

Rushing that into 15-25 seconds produces videos that feel shallow. Going 60-90 seconds lets the reframe land with enough weight to get shared.
